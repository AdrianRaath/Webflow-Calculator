How it Works

Our VAT calculator allows you to add VAT to a number of extract VAT from a number in seconds. No need work it out manually, the calculator does the work for you.

Select whether you want to add or remove VAT

Select "Add VAT" to calculate a final amount including VAT. Select "Remove VAT" to extract the VAT portion of a number that already includes VAT.

Search for your country VAT rate

Our calculator includes rates for all countries that charge VAT. Use the dropdown to search for and select your country if it has not been applied automatically.

Enter the amount for which you want to add/remove VAT

Enter the amount you want to based your calculation off and your VAT will be calculated automatically.
